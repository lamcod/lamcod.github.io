Theme

Imagine stepping into a digital time machine when you visit my website. It's like a peaceful trip down memory lane, where you're instantly embraced by a soothing retro vibe. The pages are designed to be clean and simple, with words gently framed in almost see-through white containers. This approach makes it easy for you to really focus on what's written, to absorb the stories and ideas I'm sharing without distraction. There is more than just text, carefully selected images help you connect with the people and subjects I'm discussing.

Intent

My genuine intent with this website is to tell you about myself and add a little fun and colour to my website, at first I was going to go with a dark theme but changed it. The reason for the change is because my head is most times in a negative place so this website represents the opposite in the effort of trying to change my mood
